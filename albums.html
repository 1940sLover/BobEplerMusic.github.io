Bob Epler's Music

Faded in The Blue

Faded in The Blue is Bob Epler's newest album. It combines the raw sound and style from his early days with the quality and experiance he has gained. This album is a must listen.

Gospel Train

Gospel Train is an original gospel music album created by Bob Epler. Gospel Train is not available on spotify or amazon music but it is available for purchase.

Reviews Of Faded in the Blue

As close to perfect as I’ve ever heard!

If you like songs about ‘young love’ sung by a pretty good balladeer you’ll like this album … All the songs fit the theme and are in the perfect order … nothing out of place … the album opens and closes with the perfect song. Though I generally have a comment or suggestion for how something could be better … I don’t this time … it’s as close to perfect as I’ve ever heard … not even Frank (Sinatra) had an album that escaped my criticism … this is a first. The 14th Avenue Studios has produced a winner …

Richard E.

Nice Voice!

....has a nice voice and I do like that type of song. His description as a balladeer rings true.
